About us

Hotel Deva View Point is a wonderful facility situated in lush green locale of Chamoli district offering quality accomodations to travellers and tourists with dedicated service. The hotel is a budget category facility that provides deluxe rooms at affordable price range. Nanda Devi Sanctuary having Kedarnath Muskdeer Park is just around the corner for watching Uttarakhands state animal (muskdeer), state tree (Buraans), state bird (Monal) and many other world famous rare plant and animal species.

Rooms

Since the hotel is a budget category paying guest house we provides rooms in the double bed, triple bed, four bed, group room(8 bedded) section according to the needs and requirement of the guest. The idea is to serve a quality accommodation so that the needs of the entire contingent may be addressed to the best of the abilities of the hotel staff. Rooms are also available on the basis of advance booking.

Dining

Our multi-cuisine restaurant serves delicious three-course vegetarian meals. The food is freshly cooked with special attention given to your taste. Quality is our assurance and we also try to feed you quality food.
Special care is taken to cook non-vegetarian dishes in a separate kitchen along with Chinese and other snack items.
On special demand we also serve the Garhwali dishes which are cooked using local food items.

    • Location

    The hotel is situated in the Mandal valley surrounded by the beautiful Himalayas mountain range. The serene beauty of the place mesmerizes the visitors. All the leading mobile networks(including 4g) are available so you can connect with your dear ones. The hotel is in Mandal village which is just 15km far from the district headquarter Gopeshwar towards Chopta.
    Gopeshwar is famous for its ancient Gopinath temple which is believed to be built by the Pandavs. Chopta, another serene tourist spot is just 24kms away from the hotel. Chopta is known for its beauty and for one of the highest Shiva temples in the world, the Tungnath temple. Mata Anusuya temple is also near the hotel, just a 5km mild trek.
    One of the toughest Panch Kedar treks, The Rudranath trek can also be started from Mandal, it is an 18km trek from Mandal. The Ansuya temple is on the Rudranath trek and can be used as a resting spot for Rudranath trek.

    Getting Here

  • By Road :

    • Chamoli to Mandal is 25kms. Gopeshwar to Mandal 15kms.
    Chamoli is well connected by all weather motorable road to Delhi (465 Kms), Dehradun (270 Kms), Rishikesh (230 Kms) and other major towns of the state and region.
    Private & Roadway buses, Taxies are available from Dehradun and Rishikesh .

  • By Air :

    • Jollygrant Airport is at a distance of 240 Kms from Mandal. From there one can Board a taxi or bus to Chamoli via Rishikesh (230Kms).

  • By Train :

    • Nearest railway station is at Rishikesh which is 255 Kms from Mandal. You can board a train for Rishikesh via Dehradun.
